This robot was created prior to Metal Sonic,
so more or else it can be called as its
predecessor. Mecha Sonic had appeared
in very few games but its skills were very
similar to Metal Sonic. As the name suggests,
it was created to match the abilities of Sonic
the Hedgehog, in addition to flight and
spike projectiles. However, Mecha Sonic
didn't last for long because Sonic had
eventually destroyed it for good.

like super sonic speed and mid-air,

nuissance than Eggman himself. However,
loss is the only word that eventually
comes down for this robot, when Sonic
and his friends team up to defeat it.

The root cause for all that is bad.
Dr. Eggman, formerly known as
Dr. Ivo Robotnik, is the
only other human character, apart
from Dr. Eggman Nega, who has the
higest levels of intelligence.
As a result, he can build anything
that you can imagine; from spaceships to
large aircrafts to powerful robots etc.
He is the arch nemesis of all the
characters from Sonic's world. He
is also very ambitious, as he intends
to create his own dream world, Eggman Land.
Despite all this, he is easily defeated
by Sonic and his friends.

Dr. Eggman Nega is a descendant of Dr. Eggman,
who time travelled to the past to take over
Blaze's world. His ideas are very similar to
that of Eggman, but is far more cruel than him.
He initially hated Eggman because the latter
was unable to take over the world despite his
so called ideas and inventions. Eventually he
teamed up with Eggman to take over Sonic's and
Blaze's world but failed miserably. He does not
even think twice before acting.

Egg Robos are a class of robots that
are considered as the robotic replicas
of Dr. Eggman. They briefly appeared
in Sonic 3 and Knuckles as the boss
for Knuckles during the latter's
gameplay. After being destroyed by
Sonic and his team, Egg Robos have not
made any canonical appearances since then.
However, they have played a significant
role in aiding Dr. Eggman when he invaded
Angel Island to steal the Master Emerald.
