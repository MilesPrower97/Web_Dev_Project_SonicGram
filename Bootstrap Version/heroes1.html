Heroes

Rouge the Bat

This bat can be best called as a female fatale, because of her seductive nature and shrewd personality. A skilled thief who loves stealing jewels and precious items for her own selfish desires, she also has a heart of gold. Do not mistake her for her so-called negative aspects; she teams up with her friends to destroy and defeat Dr. Eggman's creations.

Cream the Rabbit

Cream is best defined as the most sweetest and innocent of all characters. She is equally loved by her friends, the closest being Blaze, Sonic and Tails. She travels around with her pet Chao named Cheese. Cream completely detests evil and has a deep rooted anger for Dr. Eggman. Even though she is sweet, she is also very vain and not very smart like her other friends. Her emotions get the better of her.

Amy Rose the Hedgehog

This pink hedgehog is the most wildest among her friends. She wields a large gold striped hammer to attack her enemies. She has a big time crush on Sonic and has expressed her love for him openly; Sonic finds this as a nuissance. No other girl is allowed to talk to him apart from her, to which she can get extremely angry. Nonetheless, she makes a good companion and teammate when battling out with Dr. Eggman and his minions.

Blaze the Cat

This cat princess can be considered as the direct female counterpart of Sonic. As her name suggests, she has the ability of pyrokinesis or controlling fire. She is a hot-headed feline princess who belongs to another dimension than that of Sonic's, where her royal family rules over. She is also the guardian of the Sol Emeralds, the equivalent of Chaos Emeralds, which protect her world. Initially a very withdrawn individual, she overcame her withdrawal nature when she befriended Sonic and his friends.

Sonic the Hedgehog

A blue hedgehog who is in hot pursuit of Dr. Eggman, the evil scientist who intends to take over Sonic's world. His never ending fights with Eggman have been going on since 1991. A mastero of speed, Sonic has the greatest ability to run at very high speeds in very less time, hence his name. He can also harness the power of the Chaos Emeralds to transform into Super Sonic, a more powerful state which includes unlimited speed, flight and superhuman strength. Despite his hardcore abilities, he is very kind and friendly to his friends and a fun loving individual. His best friend is Tails.

Miles 'Tails' Prower the Fox

The best friend of Sonic. Originally known as Miles Prower, he is a twin-tailed fox who also acts as Sonic's guide. It was Sonic who gave him the name as Tails, to which he liked it very much. Tails is also very intelligent as he can build planes, ships, crafts as well as invent various useful gadgets, you name it. His intellect and innocence have earned him respect among his peers, which is why he is far more intelligent than Dr. Eggaman. Using his twin tails, he can hover over the ground at very high speeds and also perform mid-air flights.

Knuckles the Echidna

Sega's equivalent of Wolverine, Knuckles is a red colored echidna who possesses sharp, pointed prongs at his knuckles. As a result, he can break through weak walls and climb up walls using his pointed knuckles. He resides at Angel Island, acting as the guardian of the Master Emerald. However, he is too stubborn, short-tempered and not very clever when dealing with life situations. Because of this, he has ended up in trouble many times. Despite his stupidness, Rouge does seem to have a soft corner for him.

Shadow the Hedgehog

A creation of Dr. Eggman's grandfather, Dr. Gerald Robotnik, Shadow in a way can be mistaken for Sonic as he is his spitting image. The only difference is that Shadow is black in color with a central red mark on his head. He was created to destroy Sonic but then switched sides after finding out about Eggman's real schemes. He can be considered as an anti-social individual who doesn't like long talks or act out of character. Even though friends with Sonic, sometimes he does have stiff competition with him in terms of who's the fastest.
