Cream is best defined as the most sweetest

This cat princess can be considered
as the direct female counterpart of Sonic.
As her name suggests, she has the ability
of pyrokinesis or controlling fire. She
is a hot-headed feline princess who
belongs to another dimension than that
of Sonic's, where her royal family rules
over. She is also the guardian of the Sol
Emeralds, the equivalent of Chaos Emeralds,
which protect her world. Initially a very
withdrawn individual, she overcame her
withdrawal nature when she befriended
Sonic and his friends.

A blue hedgehog who is in hot pursuit
of Dr. Eggman, the evil scientist
who intends to take over Sonic's world.
His never ending fights with Eggman
have been going on since 1991. A mastero
of speed, Sonic has the greatest ability
to run at very high speeds in very less
time, hence his name. He can also harness
the power of the Chaos Emeralds to transform
into Super Sonic, a more powerful state
which includes unlimited speed, flight and
superhuman strength. Despite his hardcore
abilities, he is very kind and friendly
to his friends and a fun loving individual.
His best friend is Tails.

The best friend of Sonic. Originally known
as Miles Prower, he is a twin-tailed fox
who also acts as Sonic's guide. It was
Sonic who gave him the name as Tails, to
which he liked it very much. Tails is also
very intelligent as he can build planes,
ships, crafts as well as invent various
useful gadgets, you name it. His intellect
and innocence have earned him respect among
his peers, which is why he is far more
intelligent than Dr. Eggaman. Using his twin
tails, he can hover over the ground at very
high speeds and also perform mid-air flights.

Sega's equivalent of Wolverine, Knuckles
is a red colored echidna who possesses
sharp, pointed prongs at his knuckles.
As a result, he can break through weak
walls and climb up walls using his pointed
knuckles. He resides at Angel Island,
acting as the guardian of the Master Emerald.
However, he is too stubborn, short-tempered
and not very clever when dealing with life
situations. Because of this, he has
ended up in trouble many times. Despite his
stupidness, Rouge does seem to have a
soft corner for him.

A creation of Dr. Eggman's grandfather,

considered as an anti-social individual who

competition with him in terms of
